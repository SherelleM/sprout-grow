Our Story

Sprout & Grow was founded in 2000 by passionate gardeners and environmentalists who wanted to share their love of organic plants with the community. They started with a small greenhouse in their backyard, where they grew a variety of plants using organic methods and materials. They soon realized that there was a high demand for high-quality organic plants in Raleigh, NC, and decided to expand their business to a larger nursery. Today, Sprout & Grow is a thriving greenhouse nursery that offers a diverse selection of meticulously nurtured plants that promote sustainable gardening practices. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best organic plants, expert guidance, and educational resources to help them create beautiful, healthy, and eco-friendly gardens. We also aim to foster a supportive and engaged community of gardeners who share our values of transparency, integrity, and environmental stewardship.

Our Environmental Commitment

At Sprout & Grow, we are more than just a greenhouse nursery. We are a community of passionate and responsible gardeners who care about the environment and sustainability. We believe that organic gardening is not only a hobby, but also a way of life that benefits our health, our society, and our planet. That is why we are committed to the following practices:


  • Organic Practices

    We grow and sell our plants using only natural and organic methods, such as composting, mulching, and biological pest control. We avoid using any synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or herbicides that can harm the soil, water, or wildlife. By doing so, we ensure that our plants are healthy, safe, and nutritious for you and the environment.

  • Energy Efficiency

    We reduce our energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions by using renewable and efficient sources of energy, such as solar panels, LED lights, and electric vehicles. We also monitor and optimize our energy use, such as adjusting our thermostats, turning off unused appliances, and insulating our greenhouse. By doing so, we save money and resources and contribute to a cleaner and greener future.

  • Waste Reduction

    We minimize our waste production and disposal by using biodegradable and recyclable materials, such as paper, cardboard, and bamboo. We also reuse and repurpose our materials, such as turning our plant pots into planters, or donating our leftovers to local schools and community gardens. By doing so, we prevent pollution and clutter and support our local community.

  • Partnerships

    We support and partner with various organizations and initiatives that promote environmental awareness and action, such as 350 Triangle, Carolina Farm Stewardship Association, and Sound Rivers. Click on their logos below to visit their websites. We also provide educational resources and workshops to our customers and the public on organic gardening and sustainability. By doing so, we inspire and empower others to join us in our mission to create a more beautiful and sustainable world.
