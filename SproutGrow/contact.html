Contact Us

We would love to hear from you! Whether you have a question, a comment, or a suggestion, we are always happy to help. You can reach us by phone, email, or social media. Or, you can fill out the form below and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Why Contact Us?

We welcome your feedback and inquiries. Whether you are a home-gardener, an environmental enthusiast, an urban gardener, or an educator/community leader, we value your interest in Sprout & Grow and our organic plants. You share our passion and vision for organic gardening as a way of life and a positive impact on the world. You enrich our work and our lives with your stories, questions, suggestions, and compliments. You inspire us to keep improving our products and services.

If you are ready to contact us, please fill out the form below and we will get back to you as soon as possible. We look forward to hearing from you.
