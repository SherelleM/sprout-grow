Our Selection

Sprout & Grow has a variety of plants for your garden or home. You can choose from annuals, perennials, trees and shrubs, and houseplants. Each category has different features and needs. You can find the plants that suit your taste and learn more about them by choosing one of the plant types.

Annuals

Annuals are plants that complete their life cycle in one growing season. They sprout from seeds, produce flowers and seeds, and die within the same year.

Learn More

Perennials

Perennials are plants that live for more than two years. They usually produce flowers and seeds every year, but their roots and stems survive the winter and regrow in the spring.

Learn More

Trees and Shrubs

Most trees and shrubs are either considered evergreen or deciduous. An evergreen keeps its leaves or needles year round while a deciduous tree or shrub will lose its leaves in the fall.

Learn More

Houseplants

Houseplants are plants that are grown indoors for their ornamental value or other benefits. They can be annuals, perennials, or biennials, depending on their native climate and growing conditions.
